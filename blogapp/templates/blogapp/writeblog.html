Add Vocab for your article
• Write the WORD which you want to add in VOCAB SECTION in the below INPUT BOX then add COLON CHARACTER (:),
after the colon character you can explain the MEANING of the word in context to the ARTICLE,
then press enter which will result into going to a NEW LINE, if you want to add ANOTHER WORD in the VOCAB SECTION, you can follow the same ABOVE INSTRUCTIONS again.

Please refer the below Example:
• New: Something happening first time
• Happy: It is a feeling of joy or good feeling
• Nostalgia: a wistful or excessively sentimental yearning for return to or of some past period or irrecoverable condition
Above is example of three Vocabs which will be added in VOCAB SECTION OF THE ARTICLE.

• Please note that you need to add atleast one vocab,
• and you need to follow the above rule of adding each word at new line
• and adding colon (:) in between the word and their meaning.
• Then only your word will appear correctly in the VOCAB SECTION OF YOUR ARTICLE.
